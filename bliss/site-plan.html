Overview

Purpose

Situated in the heart of downtown Salt Lake City, the Salt Lake Temple and Temple Square stand as global symbols of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, resonating with members and non-members alike. In light of the widespread interest in the ongoing renovation project, scheduled for completion in 2026, Temple Watch is committed to sharing comprehensive information about the exterior and interior renovation and the structural preservation changes to the Salt Lake Temple and Temple Square. This website fosters engaging discussions among members and non-members, promoting a deeper understanding of the Salt Lake Temple and the church.

Audience

Though the primary audience will be members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints worldwide, it will also include non-members who may want to learn more about the Salt Lake Temple and Temple Square. Also, those members with plans to do temple work for themselves or their ancestors, to participate in their ordinances, or to attend with others may be interested in the changes to the use of the building. Additionally, the audience may include those who are planning to visit the Salt Lake City area.
