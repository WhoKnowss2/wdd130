Meet the Bliss Bakery Team

At Bliss Bakery, our team is the heart and soul of everything we do. Our passionate and dedicated bakers work together to bring you the best in baked goods and service. Here’s a closer look at the talented individuals who make Bliss Bakery truly special:

Kathy Smith

Founder and Head Baker

As the creative force behind Bliss Bakery, Kathy has always had a love for baking. With over 20 years of experience, she combines traditional techniques with innovative recipes to craft our delicious treats. Kathy’s dedication to quality and her passion for perfection are evident in every bite.

Anna Rodriguez

Pastry Chef

Anna brings her expertise in pastry arts to Bliss Bakery. A graduate of the renowned Culinary Institute of America, she has worked in some of the finest bakeries in the country. Her creativity and attention to detail ensure that our pastries are not only beautiful but also irresistibly tasty.

Emily Johnson

Cake Designer

Emily’s artistic flair and eye for detail make her the perfect cake designer for Bliss Bakery. She specializes in custom cakes for all occasions, from weddings to birthdays. Emily’s designs are known for their elegance and creativity, making every celebration extra special.

Michael Lee

Bread Specialist

With a deep understanding of the art and science of bread-making, Michael ensures that our breads are always fresh and flavorful. His commitment to using high-quality ingredients and traditional methods results in loaves that are hearty, wholesome, and delicious.

Sarah Nguyen

Customer Service Manager

With a warm smile and a keen sense of organization, Sarah ensures every customer has a delightful experience at Bliss Bakery. Her hospitality management background and passion for excellent service make her the perfect person to lead our customer service team.

Mark Thompson

Barista

Mark’s expertise adds an extra layer of bliss to our bakery. He is a certified barista. Mark’s dedication to crafting the perfect cup of coffee or hot chocolate complements our baked goods beautifully, making every visit to Bliss Bakery a whole sensory experience.

Lily Chen

Marketing Coordinator

With a degree in marketing and a talent for social media, Lily manages our online presence and promotional campaigns. Lily’s engaging content and community outreach efforts ensure that our bakery remains a beloved local favorite and continues to attract new customers.

Carlos Martinez

Decorator

Carlos’s artistic talent shines through in every treat he decorates at Bliss Bakery with a touch of blissful magic. Whether it’s intricate icing on cookies or elaborate designs on cakes, his attention to detail and artistic flair make our baked goods visually stunning.

Our Support Team behind the scenes: Our support team includes bakers, decorators, delivery drivers, and more. Each member of our team plays a crucial role in bringing you the delicious products and excellent service you’ve come to expect from Bliss Bakery.
