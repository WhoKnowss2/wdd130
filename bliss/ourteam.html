Meet the Bliss Bakery Team

At Bliss Bakery, our team is the heart and soul of everything we do. Our passionate and dedicated bakers work together to bring you the best in baked goods and service. Here’s a closer look at the talented individuals who make Bliss Bakery truly special:

Kathy Smith - Founder and Head Baker

As the creative force behind Bliss Bakery, Kathy has always had a love for baking. With over 20 years of experience, she combines traditional techniques with innovative recipes to craft our delicious treats. Kathy’s dedication to quality and her passion for perfection are evident in every bite.

Anna Rodriguez - Pastry Chef

Anna brings her expertise in pastry arts to Bliss Bakery. A graduate of the renowned Culinary Institute of America, she has worked in some of the finest bakeries in the country. Her creativity and attention to detail 4ensure that our pastries are not only beautiful but also irresistibly tasty.

Emily Johnson - Cake Designer

Emily’s artistic flair and eye for detail make her the perfect cake designer for Bliss Bakery. She specializes in custom cakes for all occasions, from weddings to birthdays. Emily’s designs are known for their elegance and creativity, making every celebration extra special.

Michael Lee - Bread Specialist

With a deep understanding of the art and science of bread-making, Michael ensures that our breads are always fresh and flavorful. His commitment to using high-quality ingredients and traditional methods results in loaves that are hearty, wholesome, and delicious.

Our Support Team behind the scenes: Our support team includes bakers, decorators, delivery drivers, and more. Each member of our team plays a crucial role in bringing you the delicious products and excellent service you’ve come to expect from Bliss Bakery.
