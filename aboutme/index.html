About Me

Kathy Christensen

HI! I'm Kathy. Originally from Washington State, I now live in Los Angeles, California. My three adult sons live near me. Years ago, when I was 18, I joined the church after meeting a member at work. Having gospel-centered learning as a critical aspect of obtaining secular knowledge is the perfect setting for me as I pursue my bachelor's degree in professional studies with two certificates in family history.

I'm an experienced professional forensic, genetic, and investigative genetic genealogist. While genealogy and family history are my passions and where many of my talents are, I also love listening to music, playing video games, and watching movies. As a foodie, I enjoy many different foods and trying new ones. One of my next adventures involves exploring and experimenting with Arroz Chaufa.

I love that we have incredible fellow students who enrich our experience at BYUI, and I look forward to getting to know you.
